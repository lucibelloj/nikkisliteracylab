The Approach

Evidence-based methods that meet every child where they are

01

Assess

Begin with a thorough assessment to understand your child's current reading level, learning style, and specific areas that need support.

02

Plan

Create a personalized learning plan with clear goals and milestones, using research-based strategies tailored to your child's needs.

03

Teach

Deliver engaging, explicit instruction in a supportive environment where mistakes are learning opportunities and progress is celebrated.

04

Grow

Monitor progress continuously, adjusting instruction as needed and keeping parents informed every step of the way.

Every Child Can Learn to Read

Reading is the foundation for all learning. When children struggle with reading, it affects their confidence and success across every subject. But with the right instruction and support, every child can become a capable, confident reader.

I believe in meeting children where they are, building on their strengths, and using proven methods that work. My goal isn't just to help children read better - it's to help them discover the joy of reading.

About Nikki

Dedicated to helping every child succeed

With over a decade of classroom experience and specialized training in literacy intervention, I bring both expertise and heart to every tutoring session. I hold a Sixth Year Certificate in Reading from Southern Connecticut State University, a Master's degree in Curriculum and Instruction from UConn, and Connecticut certifications in both Elementary Education (K-6) and Remedial Reading and Language Arts (1-12).

As a former Kindergarten teacher and Literacy Interventionist, I've worked with hundreds of students at all reading levels. I've seen firsthand how the right instruction at the right time can transform a struggling reader into a confident one. My approach is grounded in the Science of Reading - using systematic, explicit instruction in phonological awareness and phonics through programs like UFLI Foundations to ensure every child builds a strong foundation for reading success.

Whether your child needs help with foundational phonics skills, reading comprehension, or building fluency, I'm here to partner with you and your child on their literacy journey.
